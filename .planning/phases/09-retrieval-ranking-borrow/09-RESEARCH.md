# Phase 9 Research

## Current Best Explanation

The residual evidence no longer points first to missing truth logic, noise pollution, or bridge eligibility. It points to poor promotion of good hybrid-supported candidates into the final answer path.

Why:

- the main unresolved bucket is still `prompt_miss`
- the smaller `handoff_format_miss` bucket is real but not dominant
- `judge_artifact` remains explicit noise, not a breakthrough target

## Why Ranking Is The Next Bounded Bet

Ranking is the smallest remaining mechanism that can change:

- which items rise to the top
- which items survive compact token budgets
- whether higher-authority / fresher / higher-quality entries dominate the payload seen by the model

## Legacy Signals Worth Borrowing

The legacy retrieval module contributes bounded, explainable signals:

- scope precedence
- authority priority
- status priority
- quality adjustment
- freshness boost
- compact selection under a token budget

These should be adapted into Core2, not copied wholesale.

## Guardrails

- each signal must have a named rationale
- do not add signals because a single benchmark case likes them
- do not mix render cleanup or claim safety into this phase
- do not reopen broad measurement before the local ranking proof is green
