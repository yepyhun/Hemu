## Objective

Turn the Phase 1-3 kernel into a more release-ready system by hardening performance-sensitive behavior, correctness edge cases, relation quality, and proof evidence.

## Must Haves

- Hardening focuses on real failure modes rather than speculative architecture churn.
- Release-readiness evidence becomes inspectable.
- Graph-like linkage quality and abstention/provenance discipline remain intact under harder scenarios.
